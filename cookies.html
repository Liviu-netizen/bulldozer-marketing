Cookie Policy

Here’s how we use cookies and what you can control.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember preferences and understand how the site is being used.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies to keep the site working reliably.
  • Analytics cookies to understand how people use the site and improve the experience.

Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics to see what content performs well. Analytics cookies are blocked until you accept them in the consent banner.

Your choices

You can accept or reject analytics cookies using the banner. To change your choice later, clear site data in your browser and revisit the site to see the banner again.
