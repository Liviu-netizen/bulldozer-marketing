{Movesense

Decision paralysis comes from too many options, or lack thereof. Movesense comes with a simple yet effective solution, by presenting the user with only two options, each with their impact. Stay, or Move, with no in between, makes for an easier choice, less thinking time, and faster conversion. The slight difference in Card Background, psychologically pursues the visitor to the desired action, forcing engagement.

Tools: Figma, WebFlow}
